About

On August 24 2008, Phil Haack published a blog post titled "Streamlined BDD Using SubSpec for xUnit.NET". In his post he wrote about an idea he had for BDD-style syntax for xUnit.net unit tests. His idea was implemented by Brad Wilson in xUnit.net changeset 22555.

After using this syntax on a couple of personal projects, I have decided to extract the relevant code and publish it on GitHub as a standalone project. This is the result.
